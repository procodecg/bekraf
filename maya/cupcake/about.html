About Us

Maya Purnamasari and Diandra Tsaqifa Aulia are the co-founders of Cupcakes Story. Inspired by the hit series DC Cupcakes on TLC, in 2016, they traded careers in fashion and banking to follow their passion for baking and opened Cupcakes Story in Bandung, West java, Indonesia.


Cupcakes Story offers over 100 different flavors of cupcakes and ships its cupcakes nationwide.
