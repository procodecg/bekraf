ChocoChips

Ingredients

  • Baking spray, for spraying custard cups
  • 1 stick butter
  • 2 ounces bittersweet chocolate
  • 2 ounces semisweet chocolate
  • 1 1/4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • Vanilla ice cream, for serving

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Spray four custard cups with baking spray and place on a baking sheet
  3. Microwave the butter, bittersweet chocolate and semisweet chocolate in a large bowl on high until the butter is melted, about 1 minute. Whisk until the chocolate is also melted about 1 minute
  4. Stir in the sugar until well blended rata
  5. Whisk in the eggs and egg yolks, then add the vanilla
  6. Divide the mixture among the custard cups
  7. Bake until the sides are firm and the centers are soft, about 13 minutes
